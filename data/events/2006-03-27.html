Room:

Hallmackenreuther Keller, Brüsseler Platz 9, 50674 Köln

I went to Hallmackenreuther today to warn the host about how many people are about to attend. I did only talk to a bar tender though. Maybe they will again put one additional person behind the bar in the Lounge. (oliver_wrede on Sun, 20:50)

I had a phone conversation with Gonzo (one of the owners) this morning (27.3.) and gave him an additional heads up for the number of people we expect. He will additionaly/alternativly prepare the cellar for tonight. I did not have a chance to see the cellar but according to Gonzo it should be big enought to host at least the expected 65 people. (Rene Bredlau)
